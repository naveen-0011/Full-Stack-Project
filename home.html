Introduction

"When you find yourself in some strange place, hostel life is where it starts as well. It’s not just an adventure; it’s a home away from home! "

The Government P.G. Girls Hostel is located at Race Course, Coimbatore, Tamil Nadu. The college provides hostel facilities. The facilities provided for the students make them feel at home during their course of stay in the hostel. The hostel is surrounded with lush green lands, with tall green trees and garden benches, thus creating and airy and ambient atmosphere for students to move around. During the Autonomy committee visit the members appreciated the Hostel Campus as the “BEST ONE”. Every year, Hostel Day is celebrated as “Phoenix Carnival” with various gala events, grand cultural programs and delicious food.

Administration

The Warden in a hostel is the principal authority and executive in all matters relating to resident students welfare, their discipline and messing as well as the administration and security of the hostel. The Girls hostel is managed by a Deputy Warden who is a regular faculty of the College, Residential Students Counsellor (RSC) and Steward. The students can approach any of the above officers for help, guidance and grievance redressal. Representations to higher officers must be forwarded through proper channel.

Hostel Mess

Food is served from the Hostel mess and the Menu is chosen by the students which executed by the mess in-charge. The students can pay the hostel fees directly at the bank counter available within the College campus. The Hostel fees includes Caution deposit, Mess bill and EB charges.

Committees And Activities

Anti-Ragging Committee:

Ragging is strictly Prohibited: A student seeking admission to the hostel shall have to submit another affidavit along with her application for hostel accommodation that she is aware of the ragging law in this regard and agrees to abide by the punishment meted out if she is found guilty of ragging and/or abetting ragging.

Educational Activities:

To enrich the student’s knowledge, newspapers and weekly magazines are provided. Sports equipments are provided to encourage the students in sports. Facilities for indoor games are available inside the hostel campus. The hostel has an “ENVIRO CLUB” in which all the students are members, its main motive is to go by the 3R rule that is Reduce, Reuse and Recycle. “The best way to make a clean India is by bringing the younger generation into action and also by providing them insights about the hazardous effects of pollution and how to reduce them” the above words of our honorable Prime Minister Narendra Modi greatly inspired the students residing in the campus to put in some efforts to keep their surroundings clean and a better world to live in.

Recreation Activities:

Audio system is played in Multipurpose Hall for every week end. To entertain and engage the students in a useful venture, the institution arranges hostel tour every year.

Rules And Regulation

  1. Student’s should read the rules before signing the application form.
  2. Rules to be displayed in the hostel also.
  3. Compliant form/register to be provided to students.
  4. Inspection to be done by hostel committee.
  5. Suggestion forms to be provided
  6. Suggestion box provided in the hostel to be opened every month in the presences of deputy warden.
  7. Parents/Guardians should give an undertaking to co-operate with the hostel authorities and should be available on call as and when required and disclose all contact details
  8. Celebration of birthday – it shall be with prior written permission of the deputy warden. Guests are not strictly allowed.
